---

## The Problem with Mirrors

Traditional lasers require:
- Two physical mirrors facing each other
- A gain medium between them
- Precise alignment (vulnerable to vibration, temperature, contamination)
- Physical contact with the gain medium (limits power density)

The mirrors are the bottleneck. They limit power, stability, and scalability.

---

## The Solution: Quantum Interference Mirrors

QIMs use the **quantum interference of coherent light** to create feedback without physical mirrors:

1. A high-density ensemble of NV-diamond centers acts as the gain medium
2. Coherent light pulses create standing wave patterns through interference
3. The interference pattern itself becomes the "mirror" — reflecting light through phase cancellation and reinforcement
4. No physical contact. No alignment issues. No thermal distortion.
