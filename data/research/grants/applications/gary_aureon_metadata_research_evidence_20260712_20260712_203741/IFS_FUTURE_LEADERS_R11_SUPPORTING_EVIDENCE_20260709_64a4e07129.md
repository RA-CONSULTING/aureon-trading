## Proposed Fellowship Theme

**Aureon Evidence OS for AI-Driven Scientific Discovery and Auditable SME Automation**

The proposed fellowship would build a local-first evidence operating system that turns research outputs, software repositories, and operational workflow records into reproducible, reviewer-ready evidence.

The programme has two linked themes:

1. AI-driven scientific discovery workflows that organise source material, classify claims, support reproducible experiments, and generate transparent technical reports.
2. SME automation workflows that make business evidence, compliance records, and operational decisions easier to review, audit, and reuse.
