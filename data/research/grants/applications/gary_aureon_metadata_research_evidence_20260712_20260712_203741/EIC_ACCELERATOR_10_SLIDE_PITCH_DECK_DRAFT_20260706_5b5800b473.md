# EIC Accelerator 10-Slide Pitch Deck Draft

Format target: up to 10 slides in PDF format.

## 1. Vision

Aureon Evidence Studio makes AI-assisted creative work provable, reviewable and reusable.

## 2. Customer Pain

SMEs lose time rebuilding source trails, approvals and evidence after AI-enabled work is complete.

## 3. Product

Local-first evidence routing, claim-to-source mapping, completeness scoring and human review gates.

## 4. Why Now

AI adoption is accelerating while customer, IP, privacy and funder proof expectations are rising.

## 5. Innovation

The value is not another content generator; it is the proof layer around AI-enabled production.

## 6. Market

Beachhead: advertising, marketing, Createch and digital service SMEs. Expansion: wider creative industries.

## 7. Business Model

Setup support, monthly access, evidence-pack generation and premium workflow assurance.

## 8. Validation Plan

Pilot workflows, time-saved metrics, completeness scoring, user feedback and privacy/IP review.

## 9. Team

Gary Leckey and R&A Consulting lead with targeted software, UX, privacy and market advisors.

## 10. Ask

Prepare EIC Step 1 only after pilot evidence, LOIs, FTO and finance plan are complete.
