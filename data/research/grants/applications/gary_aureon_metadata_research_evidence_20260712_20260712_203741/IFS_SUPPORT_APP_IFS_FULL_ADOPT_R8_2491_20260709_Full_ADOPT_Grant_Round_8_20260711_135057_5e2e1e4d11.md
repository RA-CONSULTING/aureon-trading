## Project Summary

The project will package Aureon's evidence capture and reporting approach for farmer-led trial documentation, helping an eligible applicant maintain clear records of scope, observations, costs, findings and practical adoption decisions.

## Planned Work Packages

- WP1: Confirm eligible applicant, trial problem statement, collaborator roles and practical success measures.
- WP2: Configure evidence capture for observations, costs, decisions and implementation learning.
- WP3: Produce trial report materials and reusable evidence templates for adoption decisions.
