## Innovation

Aureon Evidence OS is a local-first approach to evidence management for innovation projects. It is designed to improve how project teams collect, organise, verify, and report technical evidence during research, development, trial, and deployment activity. The proposed work package focuses on practical assurance: source inventories, traceable claims, reproducible records, risk tagging, and structured reporting outputs that can support partner review and funder assessment.

## Project Need

Collaborative innovation projects often generate evidence across documents, repositories, test records, partner notes, and operational systems. Without a controlled evidence process, project teams can lose traceability between technical claims, delivery activity, and the material needed for review. Aureon addresses this need by providing a structured evidence workflow that keeps project claims connected to source records and prepares clear reporting outputs for partners and funders.
