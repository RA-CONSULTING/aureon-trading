## Grant Use Rules

1. Use Companies House and GitHub as Class A evidence for legal/software facts.
2. Use ResearchGate, Academia, and ORCID as Class B public profile/dissemination evidence unless independently verified by a funder, university, DOI, publisher, or peer-review source.
3. Do not quote trading success, guaranteed performance, "100% success", energy, consciousness, defence, or reality-engineering claims in formal applications.
4. Translate internal language into funder-safe terms:
   - HNC: coherence-scoring and evidence-routing hypothesis.
   - daemon: background service or autonomous software agent.
   - Queen/mind hub: orchestration console or workflow controller.
   - reality engineering: simulation-based modelling of complex systems, or omit.
5. Before any external submission, confirm:
   - Companies House confirmation statement status.
   - exact applicant and organisation details.
   - subsidy history and project costs.
   - ORCID/Academia/ResearchGate identifiers if used in the form.
