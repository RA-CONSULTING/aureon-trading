### Project summary - project_summary_200_words
- Status: DRAFT_READY_KB_WORK_PACKAGE_REQUIRED

R&A Consulting and Brokerage Services Ltd is developing Aureon Evidence OS, a local-first workflow for evidence registers, claim-to-source mapping, reproducible records, risk tagging and review packs. The identified need is that small businesses preparing regulated, technical or grant-funded work often hold evidence across documents, screenshots, ledgers, repositories and partner records, making it hard to prove which claims are supported. The AKT project would pair R&A with an eligible Knowledge Base to evaluate the system in a short, structured validation sprint. The Knowledge Base would help define the method, test the workflow against controlled SME use cases, assess evidence quality and produce a repeatable knowledge-transfer model. The expected opportunity is a clearer evidence-assurance process for SME innovation projects, reducing unsupported claims and improving readiness for funder, partner and customer review.
