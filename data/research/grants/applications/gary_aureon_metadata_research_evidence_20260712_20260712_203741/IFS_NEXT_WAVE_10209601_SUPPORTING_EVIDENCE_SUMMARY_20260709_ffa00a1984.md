## Project Summary

Aureon Evidence Studio is a prototype digital platform for AI-enabled advertising and marketing workflows. It helps creative and digital service teams turn scattered production records into source-grounded, reviewer-ready evidence packs.

The project addresses a growing Createch problem: AI-assisted production creates briefs, prompts, generated assets, reference material, code, screenshots, client approvals, compliance notes, performance assumptions, and partner files across multiple systems. Teams need a practical way to show what was produced, what sources informed it, what changed, what was reviewed, and what can be reused safely.

## Innovation

The innovation is a controlled evidence layer for creative production workflows. It will:

- ingest mixed source material;
- classify evidence types;
- identify sensitive items;
- map claims to supporting files;
- score evidence completeness;
- detect version drift;
- produce human-reviewed client and funder packs.

The project is not a campaign-content generator. It creates the proof layer around AI-enabled production.

## Applicant Capability

R&A Consulting has an existing Aureon software and research corpus covering evidence automation, source ledgers, workflow control, human review patterns, and structured reporting. This gives the project a practical starting base and reduces technical start-up risk.

The lead role combines product direction, technical architecture, evidence model design, prototype testing, reporting, and commercial planning. Targeted external support may be used for UX, privacy/IP review, software validation, and Createch market feedback.

## Market And Impact

The target market is UK advertising and marketing SMEs, creative technology teams, technical producers, and adjacent creative organisations using AI-assisted workflows.

Expected benefits include:

- reduced manual evidence-pack preparation time;
- clearer source and approval records;
- safer reuse of creative material;
- stronger client and funder confidence;
- improved transparency around AI-assisted production.

## Governance

The project uses controlled synthetic or sanitised workflow examples unless explicit permission and data controls are in place. Sensitive client material, private financial records, credentials, and personal files are excluded from external packs.

Evidence scores are framed as review-support indicators. They link to visible sources and reviewer notes rather than replacing professional judgement.
