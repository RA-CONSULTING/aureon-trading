## Fit Assessment

Current fit: conditional.

The opportunity is framed around industrial research aligned to Createch innovation. Aureon should only pursue this if the project is framed around a credible creative-technology or AI-enabled content/workflow angle, not the whole broad Aureon system.

## Best Possible Aureon Angle

Project title:

Aureon Evidence Studio: AI-Assisted Proof, Grant, and Research Packaging for SMEs and Independent Creators

Project concept:

A local-first evidence studio that helps innovation-led SMEs and creators assemble grant applications, source registers, media/research evidence, communications logs, and AI-generated drafts with traceability, approval gates, and safe publishing controls.

## Why It Might Fit

- Createch angle: structured evidence and content production for innovation storytelling, grant media, explainers, and proposal packaging.
- AI angle: AI-assisted drafting, source linking, and validation.
- SME angle: reduces friction in creating credible funding and investor materials.

## Why It Might Not Fit

- Aureon in its current broad form is too diffuse for one competition.
- Trading, speculative HNC theory, and defence-adjacent material may weaken the Createch story.
- Needs a clean creative-industry customer or pilot use case.

## Go Criteria

Proceed only if all are true by 10 July 2026:

1. A clear Createch user is defined.
2. Project scope is narrowed to evidence/content workflow, not all Aureon capabilities.
3. Compliance blocker is resolved or an acceptable explanation is ready.
4. A 400-word summary and budget outline can be produced.
5. Gary approves the product framing.

## Recommendation

Soft no-go until the Invest NI advisor call. Keep it in the pipeline, but do not let it distract from Invest NI R&D and EIC/Eurostars preparation unless the Createch scope becomes concrete quickly.
