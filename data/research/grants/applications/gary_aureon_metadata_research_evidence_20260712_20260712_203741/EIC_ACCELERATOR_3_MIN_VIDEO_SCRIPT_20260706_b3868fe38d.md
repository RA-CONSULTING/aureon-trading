# EIC Accelerator 3-Minute Video Script Draft

Status: draft script only; record after customer evidence and prototype visuals are ready.

0:00-0:20 - Founder and problem
I am Gary Anthony Leckey, founder of R&A Consulting and Brokerage Services Ltd in Belfast. We are building Aureon Evidence Studio because AI-assisted creative work is moving faster than the evidence practices around it.

0:20-0:55 - Customer pain
Small creative and digital teams now produce work from briefs, prompts, generated assets, screenshots, code, client approvals and source material spread across many tools. When they need to prove what was produced, what sources supported it, what changed, and what can be safely reused, they rebuild the trail manually.

0:55-1:35 - Product and innovation
Aureon Evidence Studio is a local-first evidence automation platform. It maps claims to sources, classifies evidence, checks completeness, flags sensitive material, detects version drift and creates human-reviewed evidence packs. It is not another AI content generator. It is the proof layer around AI-enabled production.

1:35-2:10 - Market and impact
Our first market is advertising, marketing, Createch and digital service SMEs that need client-ready reporting and safer AI workflows. The goal is to cut manual evidence-pack preparation time by 30 to 50 percent while improving transparency, review quality and reuse confidence.

2:10-2:45 - Team and execution
R&A Consulting already has the Aureon software and research corpus, an active grant operating layer, evidence registers, project plans and risk controls. The next step is to turn this into a validated demonstrator with pilot users, privacy and IP review, and a commercial launch plan.

2:45-3:00 - EIC fit
EIC support would help convert a strong internal capability into a scalable Createch product with international potential. We are preparing Step 1 after closing the evidence gaps needed for a credible, high-growth proposal.
