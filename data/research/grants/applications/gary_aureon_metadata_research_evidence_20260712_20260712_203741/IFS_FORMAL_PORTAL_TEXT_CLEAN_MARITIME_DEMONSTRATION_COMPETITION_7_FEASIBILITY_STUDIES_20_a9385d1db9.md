## Public Description

R&A Consulting and Brokerage Services Ltd will contribute Aureon Evidence OS as an evidence automation and assurance work package for a collaborative innovation project. The work package will help the project team organise source materials, maintain reproducible workflow records, classify project claims, prepare benchmark or deployment evidence, and produce funder-ready audit trails.

## Project Summary

The project will use Aureon Evidence OS to prepare auditable feasibility evidence, decision records, technical claims, and funder reporting outputs. Aureon will not claim maritime assets, site control, or feasibility authority unless supplied by the lead partner.

## Innovation

Aureon Evidence OS is a local-first approach to evidence management for innovation projects. It is designed to improve how project teams collect, organise, verify, and report technical evidence during research, development, trial, and deployment activity. The proposed work package focuses on practical assurance: source inventories, traceable claims, reproducible records, risk tagging, and structured reporting outputs that can support partner review and funder assessment.

## Project Need

Collaborative innovation projects often generate evidence across documents, repositories, test records, partner notes, and operational systems. Without a controlled evidence process, project teams can lose traceability between technical claims, delivery activity, and the material needed for review. Aureon addresses this need by providing a structured evidence workflow that keeps project claims connected to source records and prepares clear reporting outputs for partners and funders.

## Aureon Role

Aureon provides evidence capture, feasibility reporting, risk tagging, and funder-ready audit trails for maritime feasibility studies.

The work package will cover:

- Evidence source inventory and register setup
- Claim classification and source mapping
- Reproducible workflow record design
- Technical evidence pack preparation
- Risk and assumption tagging
- Partner review pack assembly
- Funder-ready audit trail preparation

## Work Packages

WP1 - Evidence architecture and onboarding: define the evidence register, project claim taxonomy, source categories, and partner review process.

WP2 - Workflow records and source traceability: create repeatable records for technical activity, benchmark reporting, trial evidence, deployment milestones, or partner-supplied project data.

WP3 - Assurance and reporting outputs: prepare structured evidence summaries, risk notes, partner review materials, and funder-ready reporting outputs.

WP4 - Final evidence pack: assemble the project evidence pack, audit trail, and controlled source references for the lead applicant or consortium.

## Expected Outputs

- Project evidence register
- Claim-to-source map
- Reproducible workflow record template
- Technical reporting pack
- Risk and assumption register
- Partner review pack
- Final evidence and audit trail pack
