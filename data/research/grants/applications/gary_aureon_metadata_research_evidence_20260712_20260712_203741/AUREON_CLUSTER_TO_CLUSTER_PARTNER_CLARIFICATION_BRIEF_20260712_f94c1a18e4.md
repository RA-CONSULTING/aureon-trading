## Purpose

R&A Consulting and Brokerage Services Ltd is assessing whether Aureon Evidence OS can support a Shared Island cluster-to-cluster project as a Northern Ireland digital evidence and open-source AI workflow partner.

## Project Concept

Aureon Evidence OS is a local-first workflow for evidence-heavy innovation operations. It converts research material, public records, grant requirements and project documents into traceable metadata, cost bases, validation packs and audit-ready support material. The proposed cluster use case is a cross-border evidence and assurance workflow for SMEs and innovation clusters that need repeatable R&D evidence, grant-readiness, partner due diligence and compliance documentation.

## Proposed NI Role

- Provide the Aureon evidence workflow and open-source metadata method.
- Build partner-ready research, funding and validation packs for cluster members.
- Support reproducible evidence classification and source traceability.
- Produce non-sensitive demonstration outputs using public or partner-approved data.
