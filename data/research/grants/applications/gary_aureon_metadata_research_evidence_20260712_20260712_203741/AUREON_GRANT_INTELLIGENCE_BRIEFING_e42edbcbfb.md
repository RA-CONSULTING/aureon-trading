## 4. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### A. Establish a University Partnership Within 60 Days
This is your #1 blocker. Without it, you cannot access UKRI fellowships, Wellcome grants, or Leverhulme grants. The quickest path:
- **Honorary Research Fellow** status at QUB or Ulster
- **Visiting Researcher** at a mainland university
- **Co-PI on a joint grant** with an established academic

### B. Consider a PhD or Professional Doctorate
Your research is PhD-level. A formal qualification would unlock:
- UKRI fellowships
- NERC/MRC independent research positions
- Academic career pathways
- **Fast path:** Professional Doctorate in Engineering (EngD) or a PhD by Publication (many UK universities offer this for established researchers with published work).

### C. Structure R&A Consulting for Grant Receipt
Grants are typically paid to institutions, not individuals. Options:
1. **Keep grants in company** — Innovate UK, UK Space Agency, and some EU grants can go directly to R&A Consulting
2. **University holds grant, sub-contracts to you** — University administers grant, pays you as a consultant/visiting researcher
3. **Establish a spin-out** — University-backed spin-out company (higher credibility, access to university IP support)

### D. Build a Grant Pipeline Dashboard
Track all opportunities in a single place. I recommend:
- **UKRI Funding Finder** — bookmark and set alerts
- **Innovate UK Edge** — sign up for grant alerts
- **Grants.gov (US)** — if targeting NASA/DARPA
- **Euraxess** — for Horizon Europe
- **NatCen / ResearchProfessional** — comprehensive grant database

### E. Develop a "Research Programme" Narrative
Funders fund programmes, not individual projects. You need a cohesive story:
> "The Aureon Research Programme uses harmonic nexus mathematics and AI-driven cognition to address three Grand Challenges: (1) understanding consciousness through golden-ratio phase coherence in neural systems, (2) enabling sustainable space energy through lunar surface power and electromagnetic shielding, and (3) building safe, autonomous AI systems for high-risk operational environments."
