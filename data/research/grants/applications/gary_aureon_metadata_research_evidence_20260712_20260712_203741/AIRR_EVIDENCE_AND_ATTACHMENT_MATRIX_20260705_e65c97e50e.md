## Red-Language Filter

Remove or translate the following before portal submission:

- "100% success rate"
- "guaranteed profit"
- "reality engineering"
- "quantum consciousness proven"
- "energy extraction proven"
- "war mode"
- "autonomous external action"
- unverified CAGR or trading-return figures

Replace with:

- reproducible benchmark target
- evidence-routing hypothesis
- AI-assisted workflow
- local-first audit trail
- human-reviewed external action
- feasibility study
- experimental development
