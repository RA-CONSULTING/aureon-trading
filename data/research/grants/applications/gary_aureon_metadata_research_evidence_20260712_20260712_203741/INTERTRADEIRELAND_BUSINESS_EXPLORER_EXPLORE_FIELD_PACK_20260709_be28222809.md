## Proposed Application Summary

R&A Consulting and Brokerage Services Ltd is seeking InterTradeIreland Business Explorer Explore support to scope the commercial and technical route for Aureon Evidence OS, an AI-enabled evidence-routing and grant-readiness workflow for SMEs and innovation teams.

The proposed Explore support will help the business define a practical cross-border innovation plan, identify the correct academic or specialist expertise, and validate a focused first implementation plan for evidence governance, application readiness, and repeatable documentation quality.

## Business Challenge

SMEs applying for innovation funding often hold relevant evidence across local files, cloud folders, inboxes, public profiles, research outputs, and working notes. The material is difficult to validate, organise, and convert into formal funder-ready answers. This creates duplicated work, weak evidence trails, inconsistent claims, and missed funding opportunities.

R&A Consulting and Brokerage Services Ltd has developed the Aureon operating approach to gather, classify, validate, and package evidence for grant and innovation workflows. The next technical challenge is to convert this working approach into a repeatable product pathway with clear data-governance boundaries, a specialist validation plan, and a cross-border route to market.

## Requested Explore Support

The requested specialist support should help scope:

1. A concise product and service definition for Aureon Evidence OS.
2. The evidence-routing workflow, including source capture, claim validation, version control, and formal application pack generation.
3. A data-governance model suitable for SME grant support, including privacy, access control, audit trails, and exclusions for sensitive finance or credentials.
4. A benchmark plan for assessing application-pack quality, completeness, and consistency.
5. A cross-border commercialisation route between Northern Ireland and Ireland, including likely early users, partner types, and specialist support required.
6. A follow-on Plan or Innovation Boost route if the Explore phase validates the technical and commercial fit.

## Proposed Outcome

The Explore phase should produce a practical technical and commercial scoping note, including:

- a validated problem statement;
- a first workflow architecture;
- an implementation roadmap;
- a cross-border partner or specialist profile;
- evidence and compliance requirements for a follow-on application;
- a recommendation on whether to move to Business Explorer Plan, Innovation Boost, or another appropriate funding route.
