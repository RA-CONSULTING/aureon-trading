## Project Summary

The project will convert research and software knowledge assets into a structured commercialisation evidence pack, covering claim classification, user need, route-to-market evidence, reproducibility and deployment readiness.

## Planned Work Packages

- WP1: Confirm partner role, technical scope, evaluation questions and evidence ownership.

- WP3: Validate outputs with partner review and convert findings into funder-ready reporting.
