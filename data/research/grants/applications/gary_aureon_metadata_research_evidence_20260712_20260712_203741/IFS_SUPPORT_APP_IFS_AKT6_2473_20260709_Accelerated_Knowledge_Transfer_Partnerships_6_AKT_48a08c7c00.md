## Project Summary

The project will translate Aureon's local-first evidence operating system into a structured knowledge-transfer sprint with a UK Knowledge Base partner. The work will focus on practical validation of evidence capture, source classification, audit trail generation and grant-ready reporting for small-business research workflows.

## Planned Work Packages

- WP1: Confirm partner role, technical scope, evaluation questions and evidence ownership.
- WP2: Build reproducible evidence workflows for source classification, test records and audit trail generation.
- WP3: Validate outputs with partner review and convert findings into funder-ready reporting.
